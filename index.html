Le Hot Jazz Club Band

About us

Le Hot Jazz Club Band was created in 2016 by six members who wanted to spread the jazz culture in our city. We are a jazz band dedicated to developing high standards of personal and ensemble musicianship through the rehearsal and performance of diverse styles of jazz music. Each band member is committed to growing and contributing musically and mentally to a cohesive, lively, and tight-sounding band which entertains audiences at community and private events in the city of El Paso. We want to entertain and motivate people to know more about jazz music.
